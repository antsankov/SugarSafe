What is SugarSafe?

SugarSafe makes predictions about your blood glucose levels. It uses state-of-the-art machine learning models under the hood based on your activity.

Predict

Helps you manage diabetes better by predicting your glucose levels in the next one hour.

Personalized

Makes use of your glucose, food, and exercise logs from the OneTouch API to build a personalized model.

Stay Informed

Keeps you informed about the prediction confidence. We tell you when we are not sure.

Smart Apps

Get predictions on your devices to plan the day and stay sugar safe.
