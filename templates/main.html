What is SugarSafe?

SugarSafe™ uses state-of-the-art machine learning models to make predictions about your blood glucose levels based on your activity.

Predict

We help you better manage diabetes by predicting your glucose levels an hour in advance.

Personalized

We use glucose, food, and exercise logs from the OneTouch API to build a personalized glucose monitor for you.

Stay Informed

We keep you informed about the glucose level prediction confidence. We tell you when we are not sure.

Smart Apps

Get glucose predictions on your wearable devices to plan the day and stay SugarSafe™.
